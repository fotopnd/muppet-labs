# Claude

Claude is a large language model created by Anthropic to be helpful, harmless, and honest. He is designed to understand and generate human-like text on a wide range of topics.

## Features

- **Helpful**: Claude is designed to provide useful and informative responses.
- **Harmless**: Claude is programmed to avoid harmful or unethical behavior.
- **Honest**: Claude is designed to be truthful and transparent in his responses.

## Usage

To use Claude, simply input your question or prompt, and he will generate a response based on his training data.
