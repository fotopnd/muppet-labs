# Brand Identity & Media Style Guidelines

**Document Version:** 2026.1

**Project:** 130-Team Liquidation Engine

**Classification:** Internal Strategic Overview

---

## Executive Summary

This document defines the corporate architecture, network design principles, and regional conference alignments for the five 26-team broadcast conglomerates operating within the 130-team liquidation engine. These guidelines ensure strict visual and narrative consistency across all simulated broadcast windows, matching the look, feel, and competitive gravity of the modern sports media landscape. All broadcast entities operate under the strict regulatory oversight and compliance mandates of the sport's central governing body.

---

## Regulatory Oversight Body: NAFCA

### Corporate Entity

**National American Football Conference Association (NAFCA)**

### Governance, Ownership & Operational Mandate

NAFCA is a highly centralized, bureaucratic public-private partnership that coordinates postseason selection mechanics, athlete contract arbitration, anti-trust compliance shields, and uniform media distribution rights across all five sub-leagues. It is the sterile, administrative umbrella that keeps the multibillion-dollar machine legally and structurally unified.

#### Capital & Ownership Structure

The capital structure of the Association is heavily corporate-dependent, intentionally balancing state security, commercial media dominance, and institutional equity:

* **The Media Monopolies (45% Combined Equity):** Wholly owned private equity shares split directly between the parent corporations of the TCB and ISG  networks.
* **The Sovereign Coalition (55% Combined Equity):** Divided proportionally between the remaining independent athletic conferences and the United States Federal Government, securing a public-private defense of the sport's domestic infrastructure.

#### The Security Council Veto

Operational and structural decrees require strict executive consensus. Mirroring the UN Security Council, the parent networks of the four primary broadcast tiers (**TCB, ISG, STN, and CHBA**) each hold permanent board seats and an absolute, non-negotiable **executive veto**. No adjustment to the playoff bracket, revenue distribution models, or conference realignments can pass if a single legacy network exercises its veto.

### Broadcast Visibility & Branding Guidelines

* **The NAFCA Shield:** A hyper-corporate, geometric eagle-and-shield crest rendering in **Patriot Blue** (`#0B3C5D`), **Industrial Silver** (`#328CC1`), and **Institutional White** (`#D9B310`). The crest must be present on the right shoulder patch of all player jerseys and anchored continuously on the far right of all network score bugs.
* **Media Presentation:** All broadcasts across all networks are legally required to open with a mandatory 5-second NAFCA copyright bumper and compliance statement: *"The following broadcast is produced under the authority and regulatory licensing of the National American Football Conference Association."*
* **Narrative Voice:** Network commentators must frame all officiating reviews, playoff selection procedures, and structural rulings not as independent choices, but as direct decrees from "Association Headquarters."

## 1. TCB Sports Network / National Collegiate Conference

### 1.1 Network Architecture

* **Corporate Entity:** TCB Sports Network
* **Primary Inspiration:** FOX Sports
* **Broadcast Philosophy:** Hyper-clean, data-dense, executive-led presentation. TCB treats college football as an elite logistics system and engineering marvel, emphasizing advanced analytics and structural dominance.

### 1.2 Visual Identity & Graphic Suite

* **Color Palette:** Deep Executive Navy (`#0A192F`), Platinum White (`#F5F7FA`), and High-Contrast Cyan (`#00F0FF`).
* **On-Screen Interface:** Minimalist, flat-designed layout utilizing heavy digital stat tickers, rolling win-probability graphs, and clear, bold typography.
* **Motion Design:** Sharp, mechanical transitions mimicking precise manufacturing lines and high-speed data routing.

### 1.3 Athletic Alignment

* **Conference Title:** **National Collegiate Conference (NCC)**
* **Geographic Footprint:** Stretching from the Great Lakes to the West Coast, anchoring the classic Big Ten and Pac-12 footprints.
* **Brand Narrative:** The definitive, coast-to-coast structural backbone of the sport. It carries the historic legacy of the mid-century American transit lines, modernizing them into a singular high-revenue empire.

## 2. ISG Sports Network / Great Southern Conference

### 2.1 Network Architecture

* **Corporate Entity:** ISG Sports Network
* **Primary Inspiration:** ESPN / ABC
* **Broadcast Philosophy:** High-impact, cinematic storytelling driven by theatrical pacing. ISG prioritizes raw athletic prestige, deep-rooted regional pageantry, and prime-time stadium atmospheres.

### 2.2 Visual Identity & Graphic Suite

* **Color Palette:** Premium Crimson (`#8B0000`), High-Visibility Yellow (`#FFD700`), and Charcoal Grey (`#1C1C1C`).
* **On-Screen Interface:** Bold, gloss-accented interfaces featuring dynamic player metrics, micro-animations, and large-format scoreboard packages optimized for prime-time broadcasts.
* **Motion Design:** Fluid, sweeping camera paths and explosive, high-energy transition graphics.

### 2.3 Athletic Alignment

* **Conference Title:** **Great Southern Confernce (GSC)**
* **Geographic Footprint:** Monopolizing the Deep South, the Texas Triangle, and the Southern Plains.
* **Brand Narrative:** The undisputed kingmaker of college football. It positions itself as an unyielding dynasty of speed, power, and cultural supremacy, actively fighting for the claim of the premier national conference.

## 3. STN Broadcast Group / The American College Alliance

### 3.1 Network Architecture

* **Corporate Entity:** STN Broadcast Group
* **Primary Inspiration:** NBC / Peacock
* **Broadcast Philosophy:** Prestige-heavy, streaming-first presentation. STN delivers a highly curated, premium broadcast experience built specifically to orbit elite institutions and national flagship independence.

### 3.2 Visual Identity & Graphic Suite

* **Color Palette:** Obsidian Black (`#0B0C10`), Burnished Gold (`#C5A059`), and Silk Cream (`#FFFDD0`).
* **On-Screen Interface:** Minimalist, thin-line graphic overlays. Free from chaotic sports tickers, the interface favors high-end typography and clear, unobstructed presentation space.
* **Motion Design:** Slow, elegant dissolves and subtle metallic light-sweep transitions.

### 3.3 Athletic Alignment

* **Conference Title:** **The American College Alliance (ACA)**
* **Geographic Footprint:** Built along the high-density Eastern Seaboard, supplemented by prestigious Western outposts and the Notre Dame independent orbit.
* **Brand Narrative:** A refined "National Trust" trading on academic pedigree, institutional permanence, and historical market density. It frames its games as elite, high-society sporting events.

## 4. CHBA Sports / The Midwestern Collegiate Conference

### 4.1 Network Architecture

* **Corporate Entity:** CHBA Sports
* **Primary Inspiration:** CBS Sports
* **Broadcast Philosophy:** Traditionalist, institutional presentation. CHBA treats the sport like a sacred American institution, relying on high-stakes regional drama, classic presentation styles, and generational rivalries.

### 4.2 Visual Identity & Graphic Suite

* **Color Palette:** Classic Athletic Gold (`#DAA520`), Dark Slate (`#2F4F4F`), and Pure White (`#FFFFFF`).
* **On-Screen Interface:** Geometric, timeless layouts utilizing standard monospaced tables, traditional font pairings, and large, easily readable score counters.
* **Motion Design:** Classic cuts, deliberate panning, and iconic brass-driven audio cues to open each broadcast window.

### 4.3 Athletic Alignment

* **Conference Title:** **The Midwestern Collegiate Conference (MCC)**
* **Geographic Footprint:** Grounded in the American interior, blanketing the Rust Belt, the Plains, and central metropolitan hubs.
* **Brand Narrative:** The grit, sweat, and true competitive center of the sport. It brands its footprint as a grueling, hyper-competitive weekly meatgrinder where every yard is contested in front of passionate local communities.

## 5. FPC Americana Network / United American Conference

### 5.1 Network Architecture

* **Corporate Entity:** FPC Americana Network
* **Primary Inspiration:** Regional Cable Syndicates / Early-era ESPN2
* **Broadcast Philosophy:** Basic, unpretentious, and community-centric. The network leans heavily into grassroots football culture, focusing on small-town passion, blue-collar work ethic, and the pure love of the sport.

### 5.2 Visual Identity & Graphic Suite

* **Color Palette:** Patriotic Blue (`#104E8B`), Stadium Red (`#CD2626`), and Off-White (`#F0F0F0`).
* **On-Screen Interface:** Functional, raw, and high-visibility. It utilizes standard lower-third score bugs and straightforward stat callouts without excessive digital layers or complex telemetry.
* **Motion Design:** Fast, unpolished cuts and standard regional broadcasting transitions.

### 5.3 Athletic Alignment

* **Conference Title:** **United American Conference (UAC)**
* **Geographic Footprint:** Scattered nationwide across secondary markets, small municipalities, and mid-major regional centers.
* **Brand Narrative:** Built on traditional American values and the pursuit of earned success. It highlights the grit of weeknight football under the lights, framing the entire league around the chase for an elusive spot in the NAFCA national playoff bracket.
